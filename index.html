2020

2020: A New Beginning

The year when COVID-19 had a profound impact on the world, leaving many with extra time on their hands.

It was during this time that I decided to embark on a journey of learning something new and discovered SheCodes.

2020 > 2021

2021: The Coding Adventure Begins

In February, I successfully completed two workshops and earned my first certificates, marking the beginning of my coding adventure.

In May, life and work took a toll on me, leading to burnout: it was time to reevaluate my path.

Following this challenging period, I embarked on a soul-searching journey to determine what changes I needed in my life to find fulfillment.

2020 > 2021 > 2022

2022: A Bold Move

In a bold move, I decided to resign from my job (project manager in a communication agency), setting my sights on a transition into the world of web development as a freelancer.

Despite having made the decision to quit, I found myself in a situation where I had to continue working for six more months. This was a really rough period that eventually led to a second bout of burnout and health issues.

Consequently, by the end of 2022, I was more determined than ever to prioritize my well-being and fully commit to my transition into the world of coding.

2020 > 2021 > 2022 > 2023

2023: Business and Growth

I persevered and completed my remaining SheCodes workshops, determined to further advance my coding skill, especially in JavaScript and in learning React.

In July, I created my own business as a self-employed developer.

Since then, I've been on a non-stop mission to level up my skills, putting my heart into personal projects, working on my confidence, and diving into the ever-changing world of coding.

2020 > 2021 > 2022 > 2023 > 2024

Goal for 2024: A New Chapter

I'm excited to kickstart my career in the coding world, leveraging my journey and newfound expertise to tackle exciting challenges and opportunities.

Join me on this next phase of my coding adventure!

Thanks for Joining the Adventure!

My coding journey has been a thrilling ride, and the best part is, there's more excitement to come!
